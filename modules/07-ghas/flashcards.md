<details>
<summary><b>Q3: Define "active committer"?</b></summary>

Anyone who has pushed to a **GHAS-enabled repo** in the **last 90 days**.
</details>

<details>
<summary><b>Q4: What is Copilot Autofix and why does it matter?</b></summary>

AI-suggested one-click fixes for CodeQL findings. Turns a **backlog of alerts** into **PRs devs actually merge**.
</details>

<details>
<summary><b>Q5: Free entry point for Secret Protection deals?</b></summary>

**Secret Risk Assessment** — one-click, org-wide scan. The report usually drives the deal by itself.
</details>

<details>
<summary><b>Q6: What are Security Campaigns?</b></summary>

Org-wide vuln remediation campaigns with tracked burn-down. Leadership sees progress; devs see prioritized work.
</details>

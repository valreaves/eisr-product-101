# Step 11: Capstone — Full Customer Scenario

**⏱️ ~30 min** · Goal: run an end-to-end pitch from disco to close.

## The scenario

**Customer:** Acme Corp — 3,000 developers.
**Current state:**
- Azure DevOps for source + CI
- Snyk for SAST
- Sonar for code quality
- Some devs experimenting with free Copilot + Cursor unmanaged
- No AI governance policy
- Finance wants chargeback by BU (5 BUs)
- Regulatory: SOC2, some workloads FedRAMP
- Renewal cycles: Snyk in 90 days, Sonar in 6 months

**Your task:** Fill in [`practice.md`](practice.md) with:

1. **Opening 5 disco questions** (across products) with what signal you're listening for
2. **Recommended SKU stack** and why (with prices totaled)
3. **Migration path** — what you land first, what you sequence
4. **Objection responses** — chargeback complexity, lock-in, "we already have tools"
5. **Partners + MSFT** — who you bring in
6. **30-60-90 close plan** — first 30 days after signature
